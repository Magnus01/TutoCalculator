Website designed by students from University of Agder

The teaching methods and learning system were implemented by a collaboration between an international student group and professors from the ICT Department of University of Agder.

JavaScript, language of the Web.

JavaScript is now client and server side scripting language used for front and back end development. This language is compatible across all browsers and is used to create interactive web apps. Think of apps like Gmail, Google Maps, Facebook and the cool features they have. JavaScript is what makes these things possible, and thus, it’s becoming one of the most coveted skills in the developer marketplace today.

ICT field, land of opportunities

With globalization, ICT becomes omnipresent. Every company in every field needs computer engineering to stay competitive. Learning Javascript is the best way to take your first step in that field, full of job opportunities.
